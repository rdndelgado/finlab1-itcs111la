COVID-19 INFORMATIONS
PREVENTION

Masks with a tight fit can help prevent the infection from spreading from the person wearing it to others. COVID-19 is not protected by masks alone; they must be used in conjunction with physical separation and hand cleanliness.

Follow your local health authority's recommendations.

PREVENTION
  • Even if they don't appear to be unwell, keep a safe distance from them (at least 1 meter).
  • When you're out in public, especially inside or when physical separation isn't possible, wear a mask.
  • Hands should be washed often. Use soap and water or an alcohol-based hand rub to clean your hands.
  • Get vaccinated and take booster's shots if possible to add additional protection againsts COVID-19.
  • When you cough or sneeze, cover your nose and mouth with your bent elbow or a tissue.
  • Stay at home, if you're sick or feel unwell.
